01 About

Academic & Research Profile

Dr. Soumya Pal is an Assistant Professor at IFMR Graduate School of Business, Krea University. She bridges the gap between macroeconomic policy and human development through rigorous econometric frameworks that measure how environmental change widens health inequalities.

Her research interests span health economics, environmental justice, mathematical demography, and climate adaptation policy. As Chairperson of Admissions for the MBA Program at Krea University, she shapes future cohorts of business leaders with a focus on analytical excellence and sustainable development.

Research Pillars

Climate Economics

The Economics of Climate Justice

Investigating adaptation strategies and the economic cost of agricultural delays across South Asia. Analyzing pollution's cascading effects on marginalized communities.

  • Agricultural productivity losses due to delayed monsoons
  • Pollution's effects on industrial economies
  • Health burden in climate-vulnerable regions

Mathematical Demography & Health

Constructing life tables to map "lost healthy years" and inequality in life expectancy across India's demographic landscape.

  • Spatial analysis of health outcome disparities
